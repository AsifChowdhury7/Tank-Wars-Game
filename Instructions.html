Tank Wars Instructions

Game purpose and overveiw

Tank Wars is a two player battle where each player will control a tank. The game consists of three maps, and the goal is to win two out of the three maps. During gameplay, the players must shoot each other's tanks. However, each tank will have three lives in each map before being eliminated. Once a tank runs out of lives, the next map will appear until the third and final map is reached.

How to play and win

  1. Green tank moves with WASD, and shoots with the 'q' key
  2. Red tank moves with arrow keys and shoots with '/' key.
  3. On some maps there will be power-ups including increases in speed, health, and power!
  4. Win the on-going map by shooting yor opponent 5 times
  5. Win the whole game by winning 2 out of 3 maps
  6. Have fun!

Map specifications

Map 1

In Map One, there will be walls, just like the other maps. However, there will be obstacles placed inside the walls that the tank will not be able to pass through.Try to shoot your opponent 5 times to win the round.

Map 2

In Map Two, similar to the first map, there will be boundaries and walls in place. Health power-ups will now be available. The five lives rule will still apply.

Map 3

Map three is the last map. This will be very similar to map two. There will be a cool new map!
